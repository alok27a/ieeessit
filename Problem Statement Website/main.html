GLOBAL EMISSIONS

Emissions from industries and automobiles have depleted the environment to an extent that there is an urgent need to find new methods for reducing carbon emission and pollutions of all sorts.

CLEAN ENERGY RESOURCE

Clean energy provides a variety of environmental and economic benefits, including a reduction in air pollution. A diverse clean energy supply also reduces the dependence on imported fuels (and the associated financial and environmental costs this incurs). Renewable clean energy also has inherent cost savings, as there is no need to extract and transport fuels, as the resources replenish themselves naturally.

WASTE MANAGEMENT

Waste management reduces the effect of waste on the environment, health, and so on. It can also help reuse or recycle resources, such as; paper, cans, glass, and so on. There is various type of waste management that include the disposal of solid, liquid, gaseous, or hazardous substances.
